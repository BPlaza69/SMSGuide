Honey Skip

What is it?

This is a cutscene skip, which saves a large chunk of time and also allows you to quickly get back into the action of the game.

Why use it?

It shaves off time during a speedrun. Not having to watch a 1 or 2 minute long cutscene is insanely good, and allows you to get immediately back into the run.

How do I do it?

This skip involves a wall-clip, and a death on the same frame of the cutscene. As such, we'll split it up into 2 parts, the setup and the execution.
