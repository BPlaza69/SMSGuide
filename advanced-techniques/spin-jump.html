Half-circle Spin Jump

What is it?

Simply enough, this is just the quickest way of spin jumping. All you need to do is input a half-circle on the control stick, and make sure you jump when the control stick is in the direction you want to jump towards.

Why use it?

it's the quickest way to spin-jump, and if you're getting fast, it's important to know. And you use spin jumps ALL. THE. TIME. They may be one of the most used jumps in a speedrun.

How do I do it?

Very simply, input a half-circle, then jump. However, more in depth, you should always aim to end the half-circle input in the direction you want to jump towards.
